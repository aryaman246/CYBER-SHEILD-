Essential Cyber Safety Tips

Start protecting yourself today with these simple steps

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for each account and consider a password manager.

Regular Updates

Keep all software, apps, and operating systems updated to patch vulnerabilities.

Two-Factor Auth

Enable 2FA wherever possible for an extra layer of security beyond passwords.

Phishing Awareness

Be cautious of suspicious emails, links, and requests for personal information.
